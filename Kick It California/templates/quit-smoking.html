Proven Strategies

8 Tips to Quit Successfully

Evidence-based strategies that work. These tips are proven to increase your chances of quitting for good.

1

Set Your Quit Date

Choose a date within the next 2 weeks. Pick a day with less stress and mark it on your calendar.

2

Tell Everyone

Let friends, family, and coworkers know. Their support doubles your chances of success.

3

Remove All Triggers

Get rid of cigarettes, lighters, and ashtrays. Clean your car and home to remove the smell.

4

Use NRT or Medication

Nicotine replacement therapy (patches, gum) or prescription medications can double your success rate.

5

Identify Your Triggers

Know what makes you want to smoke: stress, coffee, alcohol, social situations. Plan alternatives.

6

Stay Busy & Active

Exercise reduces cravings and stress. Keep your hands and mind occupied with activities.

7

Practice the 4 Ds

Delay, Deep breathe, Drink water, Do something else. Cravings only last 3-5 minutes.

8

Don't Give Up

Most people try 5-7 times before quitting for good. Each attempt teaches you what works for you.
