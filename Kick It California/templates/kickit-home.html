Your Success Is Our Mission

Who We Are

California Smokers' Helpline, managed by UC San Diego, is the state's official quit tobacco resource. We're not just a call center—we're a team of certified Quit Coaches backed by 30+ years of research and real-world success.

Real Results

We've helped over 1 million Californians quit for good. Our scientifically-proven approach combines behavioral coaching with personalized support, giving you 3X better odds than quitting alone.

Why It's Free
