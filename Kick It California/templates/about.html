Our Mission

We've helped over 500,000 Californians break free from tobacco and nicotine addiction with proven, science-based strategies.

Who We Are

Kick It California is California's free tobacco cessation program, funded by the California Department of Public Health and First 5 California. Since 1992, we've been helping Californians quit smoking, vaping, and using all forms of tobacco and nicotine.

Our program is based on decades of research and proven behavioral change strategies. We provide personalized support through certified Quit Coaches who understand the challenges of quitting and know how to help you succeed.

100% Free & Confidential

All of our services are completely free for California residents. We never share your information, and everything you discuss with your coach is confidential.
